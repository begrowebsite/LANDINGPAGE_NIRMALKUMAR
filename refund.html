Refund / Cancellation Policy

Our mission is to empower participants to achieve ₹1,00,000/month within 100 days through our structured challenge. To uphold fairness and accountability, this refund policy outlines the terms under which refunds are granted, ensuring mutual trust and commitment.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Completion of all learning modules is mandatory.
  • Maintain at least 85% attendance in live weekly sessions.
  • Reach a minimum of 4,000 prospects during the challenge and log all activities.
  • Book at least 400 sales calls.
  • Participate in 30 days extended support if required.

Refund Process

  • Provide proof of completion, attendance, and outreach documentation.
  • Submit refund request in writing within 7 days of completing post-challenge support.
  • Refunds will be processed within 15 working days after verification.

Time Period for Refund

  • First 30 days: Complete foundational modules.
  • Next 100 days: Complete core challenge with prospect outreach and sales calls.
  • Final 30 days: Extended support if requirements met but investment not recovered.

Additional Terms

  • Single refund request only; rejected requests cannot reapply.
  • Re-enrollment after quitting requires new enrollment.
  • Participants must post at least 50 pieces of content.
  • Submit all required documentation within 72 hours of refund request.
  • Abusive behavior or threats disqualify participants from refunds.
